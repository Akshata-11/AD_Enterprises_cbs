Who we are

AD Enterprises, Mumbai, established in 2019, is a trusted Wholesaler, Trader of high-quality Industrial Safety Equipment.

Our range includes Disposable Products, Face Protection, and Body Protection items. All products are made using premium materials and comply with industry standards. They are known for being skin-friendly, durable, and of superior quality.

Supported by a modern infrastructure and a skilled workforce, we ensure consistent excellence. Our team works with precision and coordination to meet diverse customer needs. We continuously train and upgrade our staff to stay aligned with the latest developments. At AD Enterprises, we deliver reliable safety solutions at nominal prices.
